about us

tvungen ha en heading

we create design with meaning. great design speaks to your head and your heart. it makes you feel differently, it makes you remember and respond.

That's what we aim for: to go from insight to ideas to design that connects. To craft things with intelligence, sensitivity and precision.
It doesn't happen in isolation. that's why we work collaboratively with our clients and with each other. As an international collective, we take a global outlook. We're open. We share. We learn.
The result. Intelligent, effective design that makes a lasting impression.

We are an advertising agency that offers our clients customized creative solutions through film, photo, branding and digital marketing on the web. We cooperate with some of the biggest companies but also smaller ones. The one thing our clients have in common is that if we do not sell their products or services, we fail. Therefore, we never give up.

We like communicative problems. But above all we like smart solutions. Creative, original, unexpected solutions that make people say: ”I know it is advertising but it is so god damn good that it doesn’t matter.” We maintain a close cooperation with our clients throughout the entire process of creating something great, all the way from the strategic start-up to the creative solution. That is the key for successful communication.
